About us.

Peak Chalk is based in Sheffield, UK and gets its name from the Peak District, a beautiful landscape with a rich history of both mining and climbing. We are able to cut down on mining, plastic and transport pollution. This is by giving you a very high purity chalk, meaning a lot less is needed when chalking up. Peak Chalk endeavours to have minimal environmental impact, so the beautiful places that we know and love can stay preserved that way.

Why use our chalk.

Nearly all climbing chalk sold is from China or the USA. Peak Chalk is French, massively cutting down on transport pollution. Nearly all of the chalk brands we tested have impurities in them. Peak Chalk has a purity difference of 0.6% to the most expensive on the market. Almost exactly the same chalk, for 1/3 of the price.

Packaging like no other.

Peak Chalk's packaging is a home compostable plastic pouch. It is made mostly of sustainably grown eucalyptus trees, and can be disposed of with your food waste recycling. It gives all the benefits of conventional plastic with the environmental benefits of paper. This packaging has also been designed, produced and printed in the UK. This way, Peak Chalk also cuts down on the production, transportation and plastic pollution associated with conventional plastic packaging.

Planting trees.

We also want to be a force for nature by planting a tree for every kilo of chalk that gets sold. We are working with 'Trillion tree campaign' to make this a reality. Just a little way of saying thank you.
