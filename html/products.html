Fine Chalk

Our original fine chalk is a staple for climbers worldwide. Crafted with the utmost care, our high purity fine chalk ensures optimal friction and moisture control. Its fine texture coats your hands evenly, providing a reliable and long-lasting grip. Whether you're bouldering or climbing challenging routes, our fine chalk will keep your hands dry and enhance your grip, allowing you to focus on your technique and push your limits.

Chunky Chalk

Elevate your climbing experience with our high purity chunky chalk. Specially designed for climbers, this premium chalk provides excellent grip and moisture absorption, giving you the confidence to tackle even the toughest routes. The larger granules offer superior friction, allowing for better adherence to your hands and reducing the need for frequent reapplication. Trust in our chunky chalk to enhance your grip, increase your performance, and conquer new heights.

Liquid Chalk

Introducing our latest innovation in climbing chalk: liquid chalk. Experience the convenience and efficiency of our high purity liquid chalk, perfect for climbers on the go. Say goodbye to excess chalk dust and hello to a precise and reliable grip with our cutting-edge liquid chalk.
