The Peak District

Peak Chalk was founded in the Yorkshire Peak District when searching for a responsible and high purity climbing chalk product. All the wasted packaging and plastic in the market inspired us to provide our own solution, hence Peak Chalk was born, its' origin seemed to make a fitting name.

Our Products

We offer chunky, fine and liquid chalk, all packaged in the most environmentally responsible way. You can find our chalk at a number of climbing centers across the UK.

Liquid Chalk

A sustainable high-purity Liquid Climbing Chalk that will improve your grip for an epic climb. Our ethical chalk lasts on your hands for a long time so that you can climb responsibly knowing that for every kilo sold, we also will be planting a tree.
